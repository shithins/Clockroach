Clockroach

Complete Usage & Setup Guide

1. The Concept & Data Ownership

Clockroach is a serverless time-tracking tool. Instead of sending your tracking data to a third-party developer's server, it writes it directly to your own personal Google Drive.

🔒 Total Privacy: All logs, employee lists, and projects are written directly to your Google Sheet. No databases are shared, and no subscriptions are required.

2. Setup Guides

For the Admin (Company Owner / Manager)

  1. Open the extension and click "Sign In with Google".
  2. Grant permission for sheets and file creation. Clockroach will automatically search your Google Drive.
  3. Since this is your first time, it will automatically create a spreadsheet named Clockroach Time Tracker in your Google Drive.
  4. Open the spreadsheet, go to the Employees tab, and add your team members' emails. Make sure their active column is set to TRUE.
  5. CRITICAL STEP: Right-click the spreadsheet in Google Drive, click Share, and add your employees' Google emails. Give them "Editor" permissions so the extension can write logs to the sheet on their behalf.

For Employees

  1. Download the Clockroach Chrome Extension.
  2. Click "Sign In with Google" and select your work Google account.
  3. The extension will search your Drive for the shared Clockroach Time Tracker sheet, find it, verify your email is listed as an active employee, and open the tracking interface.

3. How to Track Time

To record your daily tasks:

  • Select a Project from the dropdown menu (only projects assigned to your department will show up).
  • Type a brief description of what you are working on in the Task Description input field.
  • Click the green Start Timer button. The clock will begin ticking.
  • When you are finished or taking a break, open the extension and click the red Stop Timer button to write the entry to the spreadsheet.

4. Editing Time Log Durations

Sometimes employees forget to stop their timers and log incorrect durations (e.g. stopping it the next morning, showing 16 hours instead of 5).

⚠️ Editing Constraints:

  • Employees **cannot** change the start time of an entry.
  • Employees **can only reduce** the duration (e.g., changing 16 hours to 8 hours). You cannot add hours.
  • Employees can only edit their **last 4 completed entries**. Older logs are locked.

How to edit: Go to the Dashboard tab, scroll to your logs, click the blue **edit (pencil)** icon on any of the top 4 entries, adjust the hours/minutes, and click save.

5. The Admin Panel

Admins have access to an internal dashboard directly connected to the Google Sheet. Click the **Admin Panel** link at the top of the extension popup to:

  • Departments: Create, manage, or delete organization departments.
  • Employees: Add employee emails, assign them roles (admin/employee), and set active/inactive status.
  • Projects & Task Presets: Assign projects to specific departments so employees only see relevant options.
  • Time Reports: Filter hours by Employee, Project, Department, and Date. Get nested breakdown summaries and export detailed reports to a CSV file for invoicing or payroll.
