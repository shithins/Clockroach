Clockroach

Beautiful, Serverless Time Tracking Portal

Data Security & Privacy Model

Clockroach is built on a decentralized data structure. You choose where your company data is saved. No third-party servers run between your Chrome Extension and your database, keeping employee logs 100% private.

🔒 100% Client-Side 🛡️ Google/Supabase Auth 💾 Zero SaaS Data Sharing
Choose Your Database Setup Path

Path A: Google Sheets Mode

Best for small teams. All hours are saved straight to a private Google Sheet spreadsheet in your Google Drive.

Secure Web App Proxy (No shared access needed)

Manager Setup

  • 1 Click the Make a Copy link in the setup wizard to copy the template spreadsheet.
  • 2 In your sheet, click Extensions > Apps Script. Click Deploy > New deployment, select Web app, set access to Anyone, and click Deploy.
  • 3 Copy the Web App URL, paste it into the extension setup, and connect.
  • 4 Go to the Employees sheet tab and add your team's email addresses so they can connect!

Path B: Supabase Mode

Best for high performance. Connects directly to your own self-hosted PostgreSQL database.

PostgreSQL Engine (RLS Secured)

Manager Setup

  • 1 Create a project on supabase.com.
  • 2 Open your project's **SQL Editor**, click **New query**, paste the database setup SQL queries (click *Copy SQL Schema Queries* in the wizard), and click **Run**.
  • 3 Go to **Authentication > Providers > Email** in the Supabase sidebar and toggle off Confirm email (click Save).
  • 4 Go to **Project Settings > API**, copy your **Project URL** and public **`anon` Key**, and paste them into the wizard.
  • 5 Click **Connect**, register your account, and invite your team using the **Workspace Invite Code** in your Admin Panel.

How Employees Get Connected

Once the manager has configured the workspace database backend, employees can connect in seconds:

If using Google Sheets:

  1. Provide employees with your **Web App URL** (acts as invite code).
  2. Employees paste the URL and their email into the login form.
  3. They click sign in—no Google login required!

If using Supabase:

  1. Provide employees with the **Workspace Invite Code** from your Admin Panel.
  2. They paste it in the login form, enter their email and password.
  3. They click sign in to activate their workspace account.

Tracking Time & Editing Hours

To log a task, simply select a **Project** (filtered dynamically based on your department), type a **Task Description**, and hit **Start Timer**. Click **Stop Timer** to write the entry to the spreadsheet/database.

🔒 Built-In Safety Controls (Clockify-Style Limits):

  • Employees **cannot change the start time** of an entry.
  • Employees **can only reduce** durations (e.g., stopping a timer late and reducing it from 12 hours to 8 hours).
  • Employees can only edit their **last 4 completed logs**. Older logs are locked.

To edit a log, navigate to the **Dashboard** tab, click the **Edit (pencil)** icon on any of the last 4 entries, adjust the duration, and click Save.

Running Reports (Admin Panel)

Click the **Admin Panel** link at the top of the extension popup to view organization metrics. You can filter logs by **employee, project, department, or date ranges** and export summaries directly to a **CSV spreadsheet** for payroll or client invoicing.
